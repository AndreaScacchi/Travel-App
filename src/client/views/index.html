Why we need to travel?

The journey can be long or short, but it's not important! The important is to live fully every second, every situation, live the emotions every single moment!
We have to travel because we need to be happy, live with carefreeness and above all we need to know the world, the traditions of a population, the places, and this, in order not to remain ignorant.
Traveling opens the mind!!
